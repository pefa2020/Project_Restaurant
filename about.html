Description

Peruvian Delight was established in the summer of 1992 on Manhattan, New York. It all started with a couple of clients curious about the new restaurant opening in their town, then it became popular because of its exquisite taste around Manhattan. Recently in 2019, it was nominated by the Forbes magazine one of the best 20 restaurants to go in New York. Our group takes pride in the way that we can furnish our new and faithful clients with extraordinary tasting Peruvian-roused nourishment that is unique. Come visit us at 125 Main St, NY. We'll be waiting for you!
